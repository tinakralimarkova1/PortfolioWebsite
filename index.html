Who am I?

I'm someone who enjoys learning, solving problems, and finding better ways to do things. I love building experiences that people can interact with, whether that's developing games for others to enjoy or creating robotics projects with practical, innovative applications. At the same time, I enjoy the engineering that makes those experiences possible.
